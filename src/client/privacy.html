Our Privacy Policy

Your use of the Memory Dive service ("Service") is subject to this Privacy Policy and as boring as it may be we strongly encourage you to read it carefully.

  • We collect the bare minimum of personally identifiable information needed to run the service efficiently (e.g. if you have 50 emails registered with Facebook, we will just take the first one and run with it)
  • We do not read your content nor do we analyze it except, again, to provide you with this Service.
  • You can backup, at any time, all your data in a human-readable format and see for yourself all the personally identifiable information we have collected for you.
  • We do all in our power to keep your data safe. We keep our data on our service and we don't want anyone looking at our stuff any more than you do. But this safety cannot be guaranteed 100%.
  • We may be using other services for application deployment and data storage but those service have a Privacy Policy that we deem compatible with ours.
  • You will be notified in advance about any significant changes to this policy.

Please also read Our Terms of Service.
